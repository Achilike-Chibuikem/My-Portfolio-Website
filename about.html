About Me

Who I Am

My name is Achilike Chibuikem Anthony. I am a 300-level Computer Science student at the Federal University of Petroleum Resources, Effurun (FUPRE), with an expected graduation in December 2027.

I started programming on my own, building small projects in C++ to strengthen my understanding of how software works. Over time I developed a genuine interest in object-oriented programming and writing code that solves real, practical problems.

I am currently focused on deepening my knowledge of OOP concepts — things like encapsulation, inheritance, and polymorphism — while also expanding into Python for scripting and general-purpose programming. I believe in learning by building, which is why most of what I know comes from actual projects rather than just theory.

My Goal

I am working towards becoming a software developer — someone who can design and build clean, functional applications. I am at the beginning of that journey, and I am focused on building a strong technical foundation before moving into more advanced areas.
